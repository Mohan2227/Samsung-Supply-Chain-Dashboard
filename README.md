# 📊 Samsung Supply Chain Analytics Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze Samsung's global supply chain operations. The dashboard provides actionable insights into **procurement, supplier performance, inventory management, shipment operations, and customer sales** through interactive visualizations and KPIs.

> **Tools Used:** Power BI | DAX | Excel

# 📖 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Samsung's supply chain data to help business stakeholders monitor operational performance and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard is designed with an executive-friendly interface and consists of multiple pages covering different business functions, including procurement, inventory, logistics, and customer analytics.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor procurement spending and supplier performance.
- Analyze inventory levels across warehouses.
- Identify products requiring replenishment.
- Track shipment performance and delivery status.
- Analyze customer revenue and sales channels.
- Support business decisions using interactive dashboards.

# 📈 Business Insights

- Identified top-performing suppliers based on procurement cost, quality score, and lead time.
- Monitored inventory levels against safety stock and reorder points to identify replenishment needs.
- Compared warehouse inventory distribution for better stock management.
- Analyzed shipment status, carrier performance, shipping costs, and delay reasons.
- Identified top customers contributing to revenue and profit.
- Evaluated sales performance across different customer channels and countries.

---

# 📊 Data Model

The dashboard follows a **Star Schema** consisting of multiple dimension and fact tables.

### Dimension Tables

- dim_customer
- dim_date
- dim_product
- dim_supplier
- dim_facility

### Fact Tables

- fact_sales
- fact_procurement
- fact_shipment
- fact_production
- inventory
